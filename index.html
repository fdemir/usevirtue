Why should i use it?

All software as a service projects have the same basic needs. Virtue is a boilerplate that provides a solid foundation for your next project.

What does it include?

Virtue uses T3 Stack as a base. Batteries-included.
Virtue took typesafety seriously in these parts as it improves our productivity and helps us ship fewer bugs.

Like the following:

  • Authentication: social login, email/password, 2FA
  • Billing and Pricing: Stripe integration, plans, subscriptions
  • Database Integraiton: Custom models, migrations, seeds, supports postgresql/mongodb
  • Storage: S3 Integration, Local

Installation in a few minutes

Virtue provides an installation-wizard to generate your project. You can customize your project as you wish.

  1. database integration: generate your custom models
  2. authentication methods: integrate social login or email/password
  3. title, description and logo: customize your app
  4. ai generated content for your app: ai here to help you
  5. theme selection: colors matters
  6. payment/billing integration: connect your stripe account

All the steps are optional. You can skip any of them except database.
